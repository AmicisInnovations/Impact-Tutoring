Meet the Tutors

Our team of inspiring, enthusiastic tutors interact with students throughout the entire learning process. Here at Impact Tutoring we do not believe in one size fits all, instead we prefer a blend of personalised, interactive and traditional teaching approaches.

Passionate about helping students reach their full potential, he combines his academic insight with real-world experience to deliver personalized learning. Currently studying BCom in Business Management, Mikhael is also a National Chess Champion and achieved 5 distinctions in Matric, demonstrating his commitment to excellence both inside and outside the classroom.

Jayden H - Senior Mathematics Tutor

Jayden is a highly experienced Mathematics tutor specializing in Grades 11 and 12. With 7 years of tutoring experience and 7 distinctions in Matric, Jayden is dedicated to helping students build confidence, master complex concepts, and achieve outstanding results.

Mishka R - English and Primary School Tutor
